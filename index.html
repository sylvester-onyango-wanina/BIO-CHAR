Introduction

Biochar Climate Resolution (BCR) is an indigenous and pioneering carbon emissions removal company committed to tackling climate change impacts on human health, agriculture, forestry and livestock. Our mission is to reduce carbon emissions on farms and in livestock feeds by applying high-quality biochar while educating communities in Western Kenya about the significance of clean climate actions.

Vision for Western Kenya

BCR envisions transforming Western Kenya into a centre of excellence in innovation across several sectors: Smart Agriculture, Entomoculture, and Livestock Management. Our initiatives aim to restore community farms, enrich soil health, improve livestock feeds, remove carbon emissions and foster diverse micro-climates and soils.

Smart Agriculture

Combine local knowledge with low-cost technologies to boost yields and resilience.

Entomoculture

Explore sustainable insect protein for feed & improved nutrient cycles.

Livestock Management

Integrate biochar into feeds and manure management to reduce emissions and improve productivity.

Mission Statement

We establish sustainable and profitable solutions that:

  • Reduce carbon emissions
  • Enhance soil quality
  • Increase livestock productivity
  • Improve air quality
  • Create job opportunities for local communities
  • Boost agricultural potential

We aim to build a community network where everyone benefits from healthy and sustainable food systems.

Objectives

BCR is dedicated to making carbon projects financially viable by emphasizing technological innovation, extensive research and expert partnerships.

  1. Promote adoption of high-quality biochar in farms and livestock feeds across Western Kenya.
  2. Conduct applied research on carbon accounting, soil response and crop yields.
  3. Partner with local co-ops, universities and NGOs for training and market access.
  4. Design revenue models that compensate farmers for carbon removal.

What We Do

Biochar Production

Small-scale modular kilns and training for communities to produce high-quality biochar.

Soil Integration

Field trials, soil assessments and guidance on correct biochar dosing for crops.

Feed Treatments

Biochar-enhanced feed programs to improve livestock digestion and reduce methane.

Carbon Projects

Designing measurable carbon removal programs and business models to monetize removals.
